## Full description (Play ≤4000 chars; App Store description)

Orest Driver puts a driver's whole day in one place — the loads you're assigned,
the paperwork they need, and a direct line to dispatch.

**Your loads, always current**
See every assigned load with pickup and delivery appointments shown in the
facility's local time zone, full stop addresses with one-tap navigation, load
details, and live status as you move through pickup and delivery.

**Guided pickup & delivery workflow**
Step-by-step check-in for every stop: confirm arrival, capture the paperwork,
and mark departure. Split and multi-load trips are combined into one clear
route so you always know the next stop.

**Paperwork without the truck-stop scanner**
Photograph BOLs, PODs, and damage documentation with your camera — pages are
attached straight to the load and instantly visible to your office. No email,
no fax.

**Dispatch chat with push alerts**
Message dispatch directly, get separate alert sounds for new load assignments
vs. regular messages, and never miss an update with native push notifications.

**Also in the app**
• Fuel card transactions
• Timesheets and leave requests
• Document wallet (CDL, medical card) with expiration reminders
• Face ID / fingerprint sign-in
• Works offline — your loads stay visible without coverage

Orest Driver is for drivers of carriers running the Orest Express TMS.
Your account is set up by your dispatch office — there is no self-signup.
